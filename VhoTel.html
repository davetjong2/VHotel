    About VhoTel

    Welcome to VhoTel, your home away from home in Jakarta. Since 1999, we have been dedicated to providing exceptional hospitality and creating memorable experiences for our guests. At VhoTel, we pride ourselves on our warm and friendly atmosphere. Our goal is to make you feel comfortable and relaxed during your stay with us. Our attentive staff is always ready to assist you and ensure your needs are met.

    We offer a variety of comfortable rooms designed to suit your preferences. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, we have the perfect accommodation for you. Each room is thoughtfully furnished to provide a cozy and inviting ambiance.

    In addition to our comfortable rooms, we offer a range of amenities to enhance your stay. From our refreshing swimming pool to our well-equipped gym, we have facilities to help you relax and stay active.

    Located in the heart of Jakarta, we provide convenient access to the city's attractions and vibrant culture. Whether you're interested in exploring historic landmarks or indulging in local cuisine, our central location makes it easy to experience everything Jakarta has to offer.

    We are committed to delivering a memorable and enjoyable stay for each and every guest. We look forward to welcoming you to VhoTel and providing you with a delightful experience.

    OUR ROOMS

    Single Room

    Our 1 Bed Room is designed for comfort and relaxation. It features a spacious bedroom with a comfortable queen-sized bed, perfect for a solo traveler or a couple seeking a cozy retreat. The room is tastefully decorated and equipped with modern amenities to ensure a pleasant stay. Guests can enjoy a private bathroom, a well-appointed work desk, and a seating area for relaxation. Other amenities include a flat-screen TV, mini fridge, and complimentary Wi-Fi access.

    Double Room

    Our 2 Bed Room offers a comfortable and spacious accommodation option for families or groups of friends. The room features two separate bedrooms, each furnished with a plush queen-sized bed. Guests can enjoy the privacy and convenience of having their own space while still staying connected. The room is well-appointed with modern amenities, including a private bathroom, a work desk, a seating area, and ample storage. In-room facilities also include a flat-screen TV, mini fridge, and complimentary Wi-Fi access.

    Luxurious Room

    Indulge in the ultimate luxury experience with our spacious Luxury Suite. This opulent suite boasts three elegantly furnished bedrooms, offering a lavish retreat for families or groups seeking an exceptional stay. The suite features a fully equipped kitchen, a comfortable living area, and a dining space for entertaining guests. Each bedroom is adorned with a luxurious king-sized bed and comes with an en-suite bathroom. Guests can enjoy panoramic views of the city skyline from the suite's private balcony.

    OUR FACILITIES

    Jacuzzi

    Relax and unwind in our soothing Jacuzzi. Enjoy the warm, bubbling waters and let the jets massage your cares away. It's the perfect spot to indulge in some well-deserved relaxation and tranquility.

    Swimming Pool

    Take a refreshing dip in our inviting swimming pool. Cool off from the Jakarta heat and enjoy a relaxing swim. Whether you want to unwind or have some fun in the water, our pool is the perfect place to do so.

    Sport Centre

    Stay fit and active at our fully equipped gym. We have a range of modern exercise equipment to help you maintain your fitness routine. Whether you prefer cardio workouts or strength training, our gym has everything you need to stay in shape.
